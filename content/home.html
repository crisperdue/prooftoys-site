Transparent Reasoning

See the reasoning to any level of detail.

Solutions are statements, not just "answers". Proofs look like proofs, with numbered formulas and explanations. Every step is a true statement. Open any step to see the reasoning that is traditionally behind the scenes.

In memoriam: Peter B. Andrews
1937 - 2025

Professor Peter Andrews developed the formulation of simple type theory (or "Higher-Order Logic") that he called Q0. Everything here is based on that system, and the internal development of the logic here closely follows his book, An Introduction to Mathematical Logic and Type Theory: To Truth Through Proof.

That book is considered the principal reference for the theory behind computer implementations of this type of logic.
