Simple, Powerful Features

Interactive & Intuitive

Prooftoys features a point-and-click interface that makes building and exploring proofs easy and visual. Context-aware suggestions guide your reasoning, and you're free to experiment and revise steps at any time.

Simple, Familiar, and Beginner-Friendly

The system uses a streamlined logic inspired by basic algebra, so beginners can dive in without learning a new language. Proofs are shown in a familiar, textbook-like format to keep things comfortable and clear.

Powerful Yet Transparent

You can move forward from givens or backward from conclusions, making it great for both solving and proving. Every proof step is fully explorable—click to reveal exactly how and why each step works.
